Intel AI Workshop

You‘re a developer looking to expand your skills in AI? – Come and join us!

We will present the latest developments in AI both ML and DL in areas like NLP and Image Recognition along with a number of hands-on demos, where you can experience for yourself the power of Intel® solutions in AI. Hear from Industry experts as they share their experience of working with Intel® technology in their AI projects.

What technical skills you should bring along?
To make best use of the event, you should have basic knowledge of ML/DL, Python* and be familiar with some Linux* commands.

What will you get out of the training?
You will be able to work on market ready AI solutions using Intel® architecture (SW and HW). You will be able to optimize your AI solution for performance and better cost efficiency.

  • Organiser: Intel Malaysia Sdn. Bhd.
  • Date: May 22, 2021
  • Time: 9.00 AM - 5.00 PM
  • Venue: Microsoft Teams
    • To register, please visit us at https://www.intel.co.my/events/workshops

Alumni Laureate Awards Nomination

Do you know of an alumni who has achieved excellence in their chosen field, or made an outstanding contributions to society? We are calling for nominations of Alumni Laureate Awards. The award categories are:

Alumni of the Year:
Alumni who has shown exceptional leadership qualities, provided service to the community through volunteering and careers advice, or excelled in technology initiatives.

Recent Graduate Award:
Alumni who has graduated since 2018 and excelled in their chosen field, made an outstanding contributions to society or shown exceptional commitment and enthusiasm towards particular projects and causes.

Special Excellence Award:
In recognition of sporting achievements, exceptional international or national work, technological innovations, or contribution to local community.

Lifetime Achievement Award: Alumni who has demonstrated special excellence over an extended period of time.

  • Organiser: FCSIT UM

  • Time: 08.00 PM - 11.00 PM
  • Venue: FCSIT UM Alumni Website
    • To nominate a distinguished alumnus, please fill out the form at https://alumni.fcsit.um.edu.my/nominate
